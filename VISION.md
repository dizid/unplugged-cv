# unplugged.cv

**Land more interviews with tailored applications.**

## The Problem

Job seekers spray-and-pray hundreds of generic applications. Hiring managers see through the BS. Everyone loses.

## Our Solution

Paste a job posting + your background → Get a tailored CV and cover letter that actually matches.

## Who It's For

Experienced professionals (10+ years) who:
- Have complex career stories (pivots, freelance, gaps)
- Want quality over quantity
- Refuse to embellish or play keyword games

## Core Flow

1. **Paste job** → Parse requirements, detect red flags
2. **Add background** → Career history, skills, achievements
3. **Generate** → Tailored CV + cover letter
4. **Track** → All applications in one place

## Pricing

- **Free**: 3 applications, copy to clipboard
- **Pro ($19 one-time)**: Unlimited, PDF export, cover letters, match scores

## Philosophy

- Evidence over claims
- Outcomes over responsibilities
- 10 perfect applications > 1000 spam submissions
